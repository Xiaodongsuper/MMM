Welcome to VLCL

VLCL is a continual learning benchmark, providing a comprehensive and unified dataset for standardizing the performance evaluation of VLP under continuous scenarios.

Product13D

Our Product13D consists of more than 4,000 categories from 13 diversified product domains with over one million multi-modal pairs.

Cross-modal Prompt Pool

Our CM-Prompt can memorize visual-language alignment in the common semantic space via learnable prompt pools to tackle the catastrophic forgetting issue while retaining the privacy of old data.

About

Visual-Language Continual Learning (VLCL)

Our VLCL benchmark mainly consists of three parts.

  • We make the first attempt to create a multi-modal pretraining benchmark under the different kinds of continual learning scenarios.
  • We contribute a complex and real large-scale continual learning dataset termed Product13D to simulate real-world applications.
  • We propose a plug-and-play cross-modal prompt pool (CM-Prompt) module to improve the performance of existing continual learning models under the multi-modal continual learning setting.

We first collect and propose a real-world E-commerce dataset Product13D and extend the existing continual model for fitting the challenge. Then we propose a plug-and-play cross-modal prompt pool (CM-Prompt) module to augment the CLIP-based continual model, which memorizes the task-agnostic and task-specific knowledge both from visual-language without any buffer data.
